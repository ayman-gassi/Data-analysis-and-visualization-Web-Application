Visualization & Statistics

After uploading your file, you can visualize it in a tabular format. This section includes:

  • View and explore your data row by row.
  • Perform operations like:
    • Calculating the mean and maximum values.
    • Identifying minimum and standard deviation.
    • Aggregating data based on specific conditions.
  • Get a quick overview of your dataset's structure and key metrics.
  • Replace missing values

This makes it easy to gain a quick understanding of your dataset's structure and key metrics.

Plots

Visualizing data is crucial for insights. In this section, you can generate:

  • Visualize the relationship between two variables.
  • Plot data points on a line.
  • Compare data across categories.
  • Display the distribution of a single variable.
  • Visualize data in a matrix format.
  • Show the distribution of data based on a five-number summary.
  • Combine a box plot and a kernel density plot.
  • Display data in a circular graph.
  • Visualize data in a stacked area format.
  • Display the distribution of a single variable.
  • Plot pairwise relationships in a dataset.

The plotting feature is user-friendly and allows customization, such as selecting specific columns or adding titles and labels.

Console

For users looking for advanced control, the console offers a space to:

  • Execute custom queries on the dataset.
  • scripts for additional computations.
  • Debug or explore data in depth.

This feature is perfect for professionals who need flexibility and precision in their analysis.
